Most people know the Taj Mahal,a mausoleum in Agra, India, as a monument of love symbolizing the eternal love of a Mughal emperor Shah Jahan towards his wife Mumtaz. However,not many are aware that the Taj Mahal also tells the story of maternal death1 and, by extension,a hostof issues surrounding it that is emblematic of reproductive health in India.
