About Us
  Tom and Karaa have been close friends for years, and are the co-founders of FindFineTour. They enjoy the process of planning the schedule, exploring the culture, and making every trip a magical event.
  However, they prefer to travel in very different ways. Tom is a huge fan of railways and loves to travel by trains. As a physician, he doesn't get a long vacation, However, when he does, he prefers to travel to as many spots as possible. He was once delayed at Avignon, Tom joined a one-day tour around the town, and he was overwhelmed by the friendly guide and the other tourists all over the world. He then realized the different aspects of local tours. He loves to receive friends from all over the world and even becomes a voluntary tour guide to show them what a lovely country Taiwan is. He had been dedicated to cultural exchange ever since.
  Karaa loves to explore the city and prefers to stay at a location for a period of time. She enjoys joining the local cooking and dining courses, spending the whole wondering around the river with wine and cheese in her hands, finding her little secret spots in every city, blending in with the locals, and slowly savoring every corner of the city. She has been teaching after getting her master degree of education in England, but eventually threw herself into travel industry after believing the combined travel plans is the way to travel in the future. Booking travel tickets and accommodation has become rather common; planning the schedule is the most difficult and time assuming part.
  Therefore, the two best friends co-founded FindFineTour in June, 2016, dedicated in promoting destination travel plans
FindFineTour aims to:
  1. Collect the information more easily.
  2. Paint the trip by yourselves.
  3. Make each trip a unique experience.
  4. Save time in planning and make every trip a much easier plan.
  We cooperate with many famous local tour websites around the world. Our products are over 100,000. You just need to input the destination on the search bar, and then you will get many fantastic local tours to make your trip more colorful. Yes! We are a powerful Meta Search Website. Also, you can make your trip plan and even invite your friends to join. Welcome to FindFineTour!
